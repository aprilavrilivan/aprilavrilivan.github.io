Part A 1.3

  1. In this section I compared two inverse warping methods: NearestNeighbour and Bilinear. Both methods seeks pixels to be extracted from the source image, according to the position of pixels from the demanding image multiplied by the projective matrix. The difference is that NearestNeighbor Interpolation rounds coordinates to the nearest pixel value, while Bilinear Interpolation uses weighted average of four neighboring pixels. Here I listed two sets of example images.
